Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Can I bring a plus one to your wedding?
A. Unfortunately, due to budget and space limitations, we simply can’t afford for all of our lovely guests to bring a guest of their own. Plus ones are by invitation only, and we ask other guests to please not bring a plus one. Thank you so much for understanding!

Q. Can I post photos of the wedding?
Feel free to post any pictures you take during the wedding. We only ask that guests do not post photos with children unless you have clear permission from their parents. We would appreciate it immensely if no photos of our daughter are uploaded or shared.

Q. When should I RSVP by?
Please RSVP by September 25, 2025, in order for us to accommodate you to the best of our abilities.

Q. Is the wedding indoors or outdoors?
The wedding reception venue has both an indoor and outdoor section, but most of the event will take place indoors. Guests are welcome to stay in either section.

Q. What’s the dress code for your wedding?
We would prefer our guests to wear formal attire. Please avoid wearing green or white.

Miscellaneous Information

  • No iPads or tablets, please.
  • The kids' table will be provided with toys and activities. Children will also have the opportunity to play an active role in the wedding to make them feel included.
