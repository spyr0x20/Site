Wedding Details & FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Can I bring a plus one?
A. Due to space and budget limits, we are unable to accommodate unlisted guests. Plus ones are by invitation only — thank you for understanding!
Q. Can I post photos of the wedding?
Feel free to share your own photos, but please do not post pictures of children without permission. We’d appreciate it if our daughter isn’t shared online.
Q. When should I RSVP by?
Please RSVP by September 25, 2025, so we can best prepare for your arrival.
Q. Is the wedding indoors or outdoors?
The venue has both indoor and outdoor spaces, but most of the event will be indoors. Guests may choose where they'd like to be.
Q. What’s the dress code?
We request formal attire. Please avoid wearing green or white.

Miscellaneous Information

  • No iPads or tablets, please.
  • Kids' table will have toys and activities. Children will also play an active role in the ceremony!
